My Story

Born and raised in Hong Kong, my journey in web development began in 2022 when I started a side hustle while employed in the UK. This led me to explore website-building platforms like Squarespace, Wix, and Shopify, and sparked my interest in the field. After moving back to Hong Kong, I decided to take my passion for web development to the next level by enrolling in a 5-month intensive Software Engineering bootcamp. The program equipped me with a range of job-ready skills and knowledge, enabling me to become a full-stack web developer. I am passionate about creating websites that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing, and that solve everyday problems for people.

As an individualistic INTJ, I strive to live a minimal, intentional life while doing what I love: Develop meaningful experiences, romanticize my daily life and documentating all small things. When I'm not coding, I enjoy exploring the city with my friends, playing board games, and sampling local cuisine. I also love to travel and experience different cultures, which helps me stay creative and inspired in my work.
